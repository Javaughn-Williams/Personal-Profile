Personality and Hobbies

Playing Video Games

I have always enjoyed playing video games because they are not only entertaining but also a way to tackle challenges and explore meaningful stories. Through gaming, I have developed creativity, strategic thinking, and problem-solving skills. These traits help me approach school projects with innovative ideas and effective solutions. Additionally, gaming has strengthened my teamwork and communication skills, which improve collaboration in group assignments. While it can sometimes be distracting, I have learned to balance my gaming hobby with my academic responsibilities.

Coding

Coding is one of my favorite hobbies because it allows me to turn imagination into reality. It has helped me develop patience, attention to detail, and perseverance, all traits that are essential not only in programming but also in school. When facing challenging assignments, I approach them like a complex coding problem: breaking the task into smaller, manageable parts and systematically solving each one. This method improves my problem-solving skills and helps me stay organized and focused in my studies.

Watching Anime

Watching anime is one of my hobbies that helps me relax and destress after a busy school day. It allows me to take a break, recharge, and clear my mind, which improves my focus and productivity when returning to schoolwork. It also serves as a way for me to bond with friends over shared interests and discussions. Additionally, anime inspires my creativity by giving me ideas for games I would love to bring to life in the future.

Personal Motto

Progress, not perfection

This motto reminds me to focus on continuous improvement rather than being overly critical of myself. In school and in my hobbies, like programming and game development, it’s easy to get discouraged when things aren’t perfect on the first try. This motto motivates me to take small steps, learn from mistakes, and celebrate every bit of progress I make. It helps me stay persistent, grow my skills, and maintain a positive mindset, even when challenges arise.

Favourite Idea from "Top 20 Profitable AI Business Ideas in 2025"

Application in My World:

As a full-time student who spends four to five hours commuting each day, I often struggle to maintain a consistent fitness routine and healthy diet. Between classes, assignments, and other responsibilities, it becomes easy to neglect my physical well-being. Having a dedicated AI system to create personalized fitness and nutrition plans would help me stay motivated and organized. The AI could design workout schedules that fit my limited free time and suggest balanced meals based on my preferences and budget. This would make it easier to stay healthy and energized, even with a busy academic lifestyle.

Usefulness:

This approach offers several benefits: Time Efficiency: Automated plans save time in planning workouts and meals. Customization: Recommendations are tailored to individual health metrics and goals. Consistency: Structured plans encourage regular adherence to fitness and nutrition routines.

Ethical Considerations:

While AI can enhance personalized health plans, it raises concerns such as:

    Data Privacy: Sensitive health information must be securely handled.
    Bias in Algorithms: AI systems might perpetuate biases if not properly trained.
    Over-reliance on Technology: Excessive dependence on AI could reduce human judgment in health decisions.

Proposed Solutions:

To address these ethical issues:

    Data Protection: Implement stringent data encryption and obtain explicit user consent.
    Bias Mitigation: Regularly update and audit AI algorithms to ensure fairness and accuracy.
    Human Oversight: Encourage users to consult healthcare professionals alongside AI recommendations.
